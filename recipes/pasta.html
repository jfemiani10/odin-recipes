Homemade Pasta

Description

You only need 4 ingredients to make delicious fresh pasta at home, and there’s a good chance you have all of them on hand already

Ingedients

  • Pizza Dough

Margherita Pizza

  • Heaping ½ cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh bocconcini mozzarella, sliced
  • ½ cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Steps

  1. Make a well (nest) with flour on a clean surface. Add the eggs and other ingredients to the center and gently beat the eggs with a fork, keeping the flour walls intact.
  2. Use your hands to gradually mix the flour into the eggs until the dough forms a shaggy ball.
  3. Knead the dough for 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and cohesive. If too dry, add a little water; if too sticky, add a light dusting of flour.
  4. Shape the dough into a ball, wrap it in plastic wrap, and let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  5. Cut the rested dough into 4 equal pieces.
  6. Flatten one piece into an oval disk using your hands or a rolling pin.
  7. Run the dough through the widest setting of your pasta maker (repeat 3 times on the first setting).
  8. Optional: Fold the dough by bringing both short ends to the center, then fold in half lengthwise to create a rectangle.
  9. Roll the dough through progressively thinner settings until you reach your desired thickness.
  10. Repeat the rolling process with the remaining dough pieces, lightly flouring and stacking them on a baking sheet as you go.
  11. Cut the pasta sheets using your desired cutter attachment.
  12. Cook the fresh pasta in boiling salted water for about 1 minute, then serve as desired.
